Wayanad

































Wayanad, in the eastern part of the former Malabar district, is an Indian district in the north-east of Kerala state with administrative headquarters at the municipality of Kalpetta. It is set high on the Western Ghats with altitudes ranging from 700 to 2100 meters.The district was formed on 1 November 1980 as the 12th district in Kerala by carving out areas from Kozhikode and Kannur districts.





wildlife





A variety of large wild animals such as Indian bison, elephant, deer and tiger are found in wayanad.
